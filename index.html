FAQ about Perfect Tidy

What is the Perfect Tidy game?

Perfect Tidy is a puzzle game that challenges players to organize and clean various virtual spaces. It combines problem-solving skills with the satisfaction of tidying up, offering a unique and relaxing gaming experience.

How many levels are there in the game?

Perfect Tidy features over 100 meticulously designed levels, each offering a unique challenge and increasing in difficulty as you progress through the game.

Which platforms does the game support?

Perfect Tidy is available on both iOS and Android platforms. You can download it from the App Store for iOS devices or Google Play Store for Android devices.

Is Perfect Tidy free to play?

Perfect Tidy offers a free version with a selection of levels. Additional levels and features may be available through in-app purchases.

How often is the game updated?

We strive to provide regular updates to Perfect Tidy, including new levels, features, and improvements based on player feedback. Check our social media channels for the latest update information.
